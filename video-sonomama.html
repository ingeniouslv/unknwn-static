3.1 Phillip Lim - FW13 WOMEN'S "Sonomama"

FILM Words / Yamada Taro

The latest campaign titled in Japanese “そのまま(SONOMAMA)”, meaning “As You Are” was created to reflect the spirit of the season. Filmed on location right on the streets of Tokyo, the campaign stars American actress; Kiko Mizuhara as well as former Prada campaign model Louis Simonon who is the son of Clash guitarist Paul Simonon. Exploring the vibrant capital of Japan alongside their eclectic group of friends, the campaign also features ‘Black Shadow’ a notorious Japanese rockabilly gang.
